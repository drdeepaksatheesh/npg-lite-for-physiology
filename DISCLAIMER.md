\# Disclaimer



This repository is for educational and experimental learning.



It is meant to help learners understand biosignal recording, NPG Lite, signal chains, electrodes, firmware, serial communication, and physiology teaching workflows.



\---



\## Not a medical device



This repository is not a medical device project.



The information, examples, diagrams, notes, and experiments here must not be used for:



\* diagnosis

\* treatment

\* emergency medical decisions

\* replacing certified ECG, EMG, EOG, EEG, or other medical equipment

\* making clinical decisions about any person



Any physiological recordings made using NPG Lite or related tools in this repository should be treated as educational or experimental recordings only.



\---



\## Safety



Always follow basic electrical safety, electrode safety, laboratory safety, and institutional rules.



Do not connect devices to people unless you understand the setup, the safety limits, and the local rules that apply.



Do not use damaged cables, damaged boards, exposed wires, wet surfaces, unsafe power supplies, or uncertain electrical connections.



When in doubt, stop and ask someone qualified.



\---



\## Human participants and ethics



If recordings are made from human participants, follow local institutional ethics requirements.



For teaching demonstrations, obtain appropriate consent and avoid collecting personally identifiable data unless there is a valid approved reason.



Do not publish identifiable physiological data, images, or personal information without proper permission.
